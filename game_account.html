What is GAME Account?

GAME Account provides a single login method across our digital channels (GAME.co.uk, GAMEWallet.co.uk & the GAME App)


When you signing up to a GAME Account you will also have a Reward Account created and linked to your GAME Account. This will allow you to earn points in store and online. If you already have a Reward Card from one of our stores, during the registration, click the button that says 'Link an existing Reward Account' and input your 13-Digit Reward number.
